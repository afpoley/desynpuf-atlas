gridMETgridmet

University of Idaho's gridMET surface meteorology, ~4 km daily grid aggregated to cone-level annual statistics. Each cone gets the across-cone mean of the per-cell daily statistic (e.g. ``tmmx_mean_mean`` = annual mean of daily maximum temperature). gridMET ships in SI units, so temperatures are Kelvin; we shift them by −273.15 to render Celsius on the colorbar.
